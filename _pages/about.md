## Hello, and welcome! 👋

I'm currently an undergraduate researcher at the University of Toronto's [Continuum Robotics Lab (CRL)](https://crl.utm.utoronto.ca/), where I've had the privilege of working under the guidance of [Dr. Jessica Burgner-Kahrs](https://www.cs.toronto.edu/~jbk/). My current research focuses on numerical optimization and static modeling of Continuum Robots. I am also involved in developing software for the [Open Continuum Robotics Initiative](https://www.opencontinuumrobotics.com/) - a project that aims to make Continuum Robotics more accessible for all by providing open source software and hardware along with educational articles and tutorials.

I've been fortunate to receive recognition for my academic endeavors, including the [University of Toronto Mississauga's Undergraduate Research Grant](https://www.utm.utoronto.ca/dean/awards-grants/undergraduate-awards-grants/utm-undergraduate-research-grants) and the prestigious [NSERC's Undergraduate Student Research Award](https://www.nserc-crsng.gc.ca/students-etudiants/ug-pc/usra-brpc_eng.asp).
